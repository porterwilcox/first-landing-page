Work

Eduction

I'm a student in Boise CodeWorks immersive full stack course. In 2016 I graduated from Boise State University with a bachelor's degree in philosophy. While earning my degree I most enjoyed the rigorous analytical thinking that solving logical proofs demanded. I couldn't have known then that Plato's theory of Forms would solidify my understanding of constructor classes, or that the concepts frequenting symbolic logic would reemerge in programming encapsulation. Studying philosophy taught me how to develop and cultivate new frameworks of thought. I'm eager and excited to begin a lifetime of fostering my mental framework for software development.

I tend to play a lot of chess and invite friends and family over for campfires and yard games. I currently live in Boise, Idaho and am lucky to be surrounded by good friends and family.
